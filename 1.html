About Us

Founded in 1978, Sports lab has been a one-stop-shop for sports & fitness lovers in the capital. Our range of sports gear & equipment
are as diverse as the age of people whom we cater to. From a Cricket bat or a Football to specialized fitness equipments, we have it all.
For adventure seekers, we have all the necessary gear – from trekking shoes to fishing to camping equipments. Our range of products
includes some of the best international brands in their categories. We are also the sole dealer for the Tanturi – a renowned fitness brand
from Holland and Stag. For over three decades now, we have been helping people in staying fit and lead a healthy life
. While our original outlet is in Lodhi colony market, we have two other outlets, one at Ghitorni, MG Road and the other at MGF Plaza, Gurgaon
